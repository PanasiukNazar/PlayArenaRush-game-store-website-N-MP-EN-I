Cookie Policy

Overview

This cookie policy outlines how our online store for PC and console games uses cookies to enhance your shopping experience. Cookies help us deliver a smoother, faster, and more personalized journey as you browse our platform.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that allow our store to remember your actions and preferences. Whether you're exploring the latest releases or adding games to your wishlist, cookies make your visit more efficient.

Types of Cookies Used

1. Core Functionality Cookies

These cookies are essential for basic site functions such as secure login, cart management, and smooth checkout. Without them, key features of our store would not operate correctly.

2. Usage Analytics Cookies

We use cookies to collect anonymous data on how users navigate our store—what categories they browse, which games are most popular, and what content they engage with. This helps us improve our service.

3. Personalization Cookies

These cookies remember your platform preferences (like PC or PlayStation), language, and favorite game genres. They allow us to tailor your homepage, recommendations, and filters to your interests.

4. Promotional Cookies

Marketing cookies enable us to show you relevant deals, limited-time bundles, or upcoming titles based on your activity. They also help us analyze the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Purpose of Using Cookies

Cookies help our game store run more efficiently. They support better product discovery, faster load times, and personalized content—making your visits enjoyable and relevant.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can adjust your browser settings to accept, block, or delete cookies at any time. Be aware that restricting cookies may impact key functionalities like game sorting, saving favorites, or completing your order.

Third-Party Cookie Services

Our store may include services from third parties such as analytics tools and ad partners. These services may place their own cookies and operate under separate cookie policies.

Policy Updates

We may revise this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect updates in technology or legal requirements. Please revisit this page occasionally to stay informed about how we use cookies.
